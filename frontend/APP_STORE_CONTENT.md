**HoopStats Support Center**

**Getting Started**
1. Create an account with your email
2. Add your players in the Players tab
3. Start a new game and select your players
4. Tap stat buttons to record stats during the game
5. View the game summary when finished

**Frequently Asked Questions**

**Q: How do I record a stat?**
A: Tap the player, then tap the stat button (PTS, REB, AST, etc.). For points, choose 2PT or 3PT.

**Q: How do I undo a stat?**
A: Long-press on the stat button or use the undo option in the game menu.

**Q: How does Team Mode work?**
A: Team Mode (Team subscription) lets you track multiple players, manage substitutions, and monitor playing time.

**Q: Can I share games live?**
A: Yes! Pro and Team subscribers can share a live game link. Anyone with the link can watch stats update in real-time.

**Q: How do I export game data?**
A: After a game, tap "Export PDF" in the game summary (Pro and Team only).

**Q: How do I start a new season?**
A: Go to Profile > Season Management > Start New Season. This archives your current stats and starts fresh.

**Q: What happens to my data if I cancel?**
A: Your data remains accessible. You'll lose access to Pro/Team features but can still view your history.

**Q: How do I delete my account?**
A: Contact support@hoopstats.app to request account deletion.
